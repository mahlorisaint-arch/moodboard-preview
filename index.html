About Lesego

Lesego is a creative producer with deep roots in South African film and television. Her work spans DiepCity, The Black Door, The Mabaso Family Reunion, and beloved soaps like Generations, Isidingo, Muvhango. With a focus on story development, script editing, and writing, she brings narratives to life across genres.

Through Moodboard Media, she offers content strategy, event highlights, and social media creation for clients across industries. And as founder of the Female Film Trailblazers initiative, she mentors and connects emerging female filmmakers through events, networking, and profiling established professionals.
