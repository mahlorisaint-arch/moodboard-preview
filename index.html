About Lesego & Moodboard Media

Moodboard Media is the creative heart of storyteller and producer Lesego. With extensive experience across South African television — including Generations, Isidingo, Muvhango, DiepCity, The Black Door, and The Mabaso Family Reunion — Lesego crafts narratives that linger.

Beyond soaps, Lesego has nurtured independent films and champions the Female Film Trailblazers initiative: a mentorship and networking platform elevating women behind the camera. Moodboard Media is the umbrella for cinematic production, creative direction, and content strategy that connects.
