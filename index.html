About Lesego Soke

Lesego Soke is a creative producer across film, television, and digital storytelling. With a focus on building meaningful narratives while empowering emerging creators, she brings decades of industry experience to every project.

Her work spans acclaimed productions including DiepCity, The Black Door, and The Mabaso Family Reunion. Through Moodboard Media, she offers content strategy and creative production, and as founder of Female Film Trailblazers, she mentors the next generation of women in film.
